War rules

  • Always try to follow strategy sent in mail. Specially top players.
  • Never take risk. Because there is no value for 1 or 2 stars in the clan.
  • Always choose any easy base and 3 star it.
  • Breaking the above rules may risk your stay in clan.
  • Dont attack without clan castle troops.
  • Try to get best level troops in clan castle from leader for war.
  • No one will fill war clan castle except leader.
  • Always try to kill cc first.
  • Make better war base design. You can copy recomended bases from this website.

Donation rules

  • Do not spam/beg for troops after requesting. You will be donated within 15 minutes.

  • Top players try to maintain donation ratio.
  • After 5000 donation you will be promoted to co-leader.
